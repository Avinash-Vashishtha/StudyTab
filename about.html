About StudyTab

Why these notes exist, and who wrote them.

Written by a senior who cleared these papers

These are real handwritten notes and solved PYQs prepared during B.Tech — not scraped off the internet or copied from some random PDF. Every subject here has been personally studied, summarised and tested in actual AKTU exams.

Why free?

The reasoning is simple: juniors at the same AKTU-affiliated college (and across every affiliated college) face the exact same syllabus. If organised, exam-ready notes can save even a few students hours of confusion before their paper, that's worth making them free and accessible. The site earns a small amount through Google ads — just enough to keep it running.

Which students will find this useful?

Any B.Tech CSE student at an AKTU-affiliated college in Uttar Pradesh. The syllabus is standardised by AKTU, so whether you're in Lucknow, Agra, Meerut or anywhere else under AKTU jurisdiction, these notes match your syllabus.

What's covered

All 8 semesters of B.Tech CSE, with unit-wise notes and solved previous year papers (2023-24, 2024-25 and earlier years wherever available). More subjects and years are added regularly.

Are these notes accurate?

They follow the official AKTU syllabus strictly. That said, use your own judgement — cross-check with your teacher or textbook if something feels off, especially for numericals and derivations where a single step matters.
